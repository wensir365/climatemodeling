
The subdirectory DATA contains all the files with data necesary to run 
the model

+ eddy.pdat
Eddy diffusion coefficients measured for Earth

+ faruvs.pdat
Far UV flux from the Sun

+ fluxesKGF_photo.pdat
Fluxes from stars other than the Sun (details in Segura et al.
Astrobiology, 3(4), 689-708. 2003)

+ h2so4.pdat
Table of sulfuric acid and water vapor pressures as functions of
temperature and concentration of H2SO4 in the particles 
Read by the subroutine AERTAB 

+ photos.pdat
Solar flux, cross sections and exponential sums for various chemical
species.

+ primo3s.chm
Chemical reactions
